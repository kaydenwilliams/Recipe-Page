Avocado Toast

This is my recipe on avocado toast, delish

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Avocados
  • "Everything but the Bagel" Seasoning
  • Egg
  • Bacon Bits (Optional)

Instructions

  1. Cut avocados
  2. Toast the bread
  3. Fry an egg
  4. Spread the avocado on the toast
  5. Place egg on top of avocado and toast
  6. Sprinkle on "Everything but the Bagel" seasoning to your desired amount and bacon bits (optional).
